How to Order

Follow these simple steps to order your favorite meals and enjoy seamless delivery.

  1. Browse the Menu: Explore our diverse menu and select your favorite dishes.
  2. Add to Cart: Click on the "Add to Cart" button for the items you wish to order.
  3. Review Your Order: Go to your cart to review and make any changes to your order.
  4. Proceed to Checkout: Enter your delivery details and choose your payment method.
  5. Confirm Order: Confirm your order and make the payment. You'll receive an order confirmation via email.
  6. Track Your Order: Use our real-time tracking feature to monitor your delivery.

If you face any issues, feel free to contact our support team for assistance.
